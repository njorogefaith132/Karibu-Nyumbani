ABOUT US

Karibu Nyumbani allows you to book a hostel around your instituition in whichever location you are in
You do not have to go physically to the hostel to do your bookings.


How to book

  1. Click on the hostel to get information on the hostel
  2. Click the book button
  3. A form will appear,fill it then submit

Once this is done, your request will be pending until payment is done
Payment is done through MPESA. Your request will be confirmed via message/email
